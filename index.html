Our Mission

Our Cadet post was formed in 1972. It has a large alumni of local, state and county law enforcement personnel. Our cadets meet weekly on Thursday nights from 6PM to 8PM. The cadets get a diverse knowledge of the law enforcement field through hands on participation and class room instruction.

What We Do

The topics included in their training include:

  • Police history / Ethics
  • Communication and Patrol procedures
  • Report writing
  • Instruction in Penal laws, Vehicle and traffic and CPL.
  • Crime Prevention and Arrest procedures
  • Officer safety
  • Cadets Age 16 or older can even go on ride a longs with Patrol Officers.
